Switch to Safari First

To add this app to your iPhone home screen, you need to use Safari — not Chrome.

1
Tap Copy Setup Link above
2
Open the Safari app on your iPhone
3
Tap the address bar at the top, paste the link, and tap Go
4
Follow the instructions that appear to add to your home screen

Add to Your Home Screen

Follow these 3 steps right now — you're already in Safari so you're ready to go.

1
Tap the Share button
Look for the share icon — it's a box with an arrow pointing up. On newer iPhones it's at the top right next to the address bar. On older iPhones it's at the bottom center of the screen. Some versions hide it inside the ... menu at the bottom right.
2
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet until you see this option.
3
Tap "Add" to confirm
Change the name to MC Tasks if you like, then tap Add in the top right corner.
or skip installing
